Magic Inq 101

Lahore, Pakistan, Monday, 23rd March 2015, 10:34 PM

Magic Inq™ is a brand new independent video game developer based in Pakistan.

Who we are? Currently "we" is just me, Mubeen Iqbal, which makes Magic Inq™ a one man indie game development studio. However, I have plans to expand Magic Inq™ by having more people on board from around the globe. I look forward to join hands with individuals, organizations and companies while expanding Game Turbine™ and have joint ventures where we create amazing games which are fun and don't have (a lot of) social and financial hurdles embeded into them for the sole purpose of making money.

Sure we have to make money from games but we'll think of other smart techniques for that which don't kill the fun in games. For me, limiting gameplay progress using social and financial barriers and long waiting times are the greatest turn offs. Yes, in todays world we do need social networking and sometimes in app purchases integrated into games but they don't necessarily have to slow down, or worst block, game progression.

I founded Magic Inq™ with a vision to create quality games which provide the same fun we used to have in the old times on classic consoles. Think about the good ol' days when Nintendo ® games were all the rage and people actually had fun playing games. We can add the same magic to todays games be it 2D or 3D, retro or HD, pixel or voxel.

Another purpose Magic Inq™ strives to fulfil is to create awareness amongst the people of Pakistan and general masses throughout the world about the various opportunities the video games industry can provide almost everyone.

Magic Inq™ will always make great efforts to raise the profile of the Pakistani games and digital media industry across the world. For this Magic Inq™ has spawned a campaign in the form of a non-profit organization known as Made in Creative Pakistan™. This non-profit organization runs separately from Magic Inq™ but is initiated, launched and powered by Magic Inq™.
